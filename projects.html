Featured case study

Willow Road, Hampstead

A four-bedroom Edwardian house in the Hampstead conservation area, rebuilt around a rear and side extension. The site sat within an Article 4 direction, removing permitted development rights.

We ran a pre-application with Camden, prepared the heritage and daylight assessments, and secured approval first time. The tender package priced within 3% across three contractors; the build completed on programme.

Planning: Camden, approved first time
Programme: 14 months, design to completion
Role: full service, RIBA-equivalent stages 0 to 6
